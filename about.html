About the Distribution

CachyOS is a user-friendly and highly optimized distribution based on Arch Linux. Our distribution offers a seamless installation process and a range of customization options to personalize your computing experience. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced user, CachyOS delivers optimized performance while maintaining its simplicity.
